Our Courses

Explore our comprehensive courses designed to enhance your understanding of VLSI, Signals, and Systems.

VLSI Design

This course covers the fundamentals of Very-Large-Scale Integration (VLSI) design, including CMOS transistor theory, logic design, and system-on-chip (SoC) architectures. Students will learn to design and simulate digital circuits using industry-standard tools.

Signals and Systems

This course introduces the core concepts of signals and systems, covering topics such as continuous-time and discrete-time signals, linear time-invariant (LTI) systems, Fourier analysis, and Z-transforms. Emphasis is placed on practical applications in communication and control systems.
